# Reference 08 · Podcast Image-Rich Dashboard

## Why it feels beautiful
Images/portraits/covers add visual anchors and richness. Structure is otherwise simple.

## Typography notes
Content hierarchy is functional and not too loud.

## Palette notes
White/blue/black content system is conventional but clean.

## Layout / spacing notes
Classic media dashboard. Not a unique structure.

## Component notes
Hero and player card are useful examples of content-specific composition.

## Custom enrichment notes
Real images carry much of the aesthetic.

## What not to copy literally
Do not copy layout as innovative. It is mostly image-driven.

## Transferable rule
When imagery exists, use it; when not, create equivalent visual anchors.

## RaiOS relevance
Website screenshots/previews can play the role of media thumbnails.
