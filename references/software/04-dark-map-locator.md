# Reference 04 · Dark Map Locator

## Why it feels beautiful
A custom dark map, elegant side panel, real entity logos and refined floating card create specificity and polish.

## Typography notes
Very small, refined hierarchy. Text is quiet and precise.

## Palette notes
Dark palette works because it is controlled. Do not make everything dark by default.

## Layout / spacing notes
Side panel is calm and structured. Floating card spacing is strong.

## Component notes
List items are elevated by real logos and compact metadata.

## Custom enrichment notes
Map, markers and logos make it proprietary.

## What not to copy literally
Do not use dark mode unless it fits.

## Transferable rule
Specific entity visuals and refined floating panels raise quality.

## RaiOS relevance
Favicons/logos/screenshots can make lead entities specific.
