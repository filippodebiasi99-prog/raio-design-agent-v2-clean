# Reference 05 · Premium CRM List

## Why it feels beautiful
The list/table is aesthetically strong because text, spacing, avatars, pills and active row are calibrated.

## Typography notes
Small, normal-weight, calm. Little bold.

## Palette notes
Yellow is not preferred. Extract list quality, not color.

## Layout / spacing notes
Rows are dense but readable. Columns are clear.

## Component notes
Status pills, avatars, active row and filters feel like a premium product surface.

## Custom enrichment notes
Small SVG/logo details and patterned control add brand layer.

## What not to copy literally
Do not copy yellow. Do not copy folder-like shell.

## Transferable rule
For CRM/list-heavy software, the list is the product.

## RaiOS relevance
Lead queue must be closer to this quality than to generic table/card list.
