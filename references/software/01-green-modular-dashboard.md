# Reference 01 · Green Modular Dashboard

## Why it feels beautiful
It is not beautiful just because it is green. It works because the modules are coherent but internally varied, typography is small, and data visuals feel custom-built.

## Typography notes
Small UI text, restrained weights, numbers are clear without every label being heavy. Hierarchy is created by module size, color and placement more than bold.

## Palette notes
Green is controlled and brand-like. White/mint/green relationship feels intentional. Do not make the whole UI green.

## Layout / spacing notes
Dense but organized. Cards have consistent shell but different internal composition.

## Component notes
Cards, charts, progress and timer have different layouts. Avoid repeated card templates.

## Custom enrichment notes
Custom chart patterns and progress visuals make the product feel proprietary.

## What not to copy literally
Do not copy generic gray sidebar. Do not copy green as global atmosphere.

## Transferable rule
Coherent outside, varied inside.

## RaiOS relevance
Use for custom audit/score/funnel modules and non-repetitive dashboard cards.
