# 08 · Reference Fidelity Gate

The output must visually belong near the selected references.

## Required score

Every final review must include:

- Typography: X/10
- Palette: X/10
- Component system: X/10
- Reference fidelity: X/10
- Overall visual score: X/10

## Reference fidelity

Score how much the output visually resembles the quality and principles of the selected reference stack.

Ask:
1. Does typography feel close to the selected references?
2. Does palette feel close in quality?
3. Does spacing/rhythm feel close?
4. Do components feel similarly finished?
5. Does custom enrichment feel product-specific?
6. Does the output feel like it belongs in the same visual quality set?

## Score caps

If reference fidelity < 7:
- overall cannot exceed 6

If typography < 6:
- overall cannot exceed 6

If palette < 6:
- overall cannot exceed 6

If component system < 6:
- overall cannot exceed 6

If output looks like generic Codex dashboard:
- overall cannot exceed 5

If output is just a patch of previous failed layout:
- overall cannot exceed 5

## Hard truth rule

Do not give 8/10 unless:
- reference fidelity is at least 7
- typography is at least 7
- palette is at least 7
- component system is at least 7
- visual first impression is genuinely good
