# Reference 03 · Logistics Map Workspace

## Why it feels beautiful
The map and tracking system are custom product visualization, not default Google Maps. Route, truck marker, dotted line and timeline are product-specific micro-UX.

## Typography notes
Functional, compact and readable. Labels do not dominate.

## Palette notes
Yellow/black is contextual and not preferred globally. Do not copy literally.

## Layout / spacing notes
List/detail/canvas layout has strong operational hierarchy.

## Component notes
Load cards contain route/status/person/action in a compact visual summary.

## Custom enrichment notes
Route lines, markers and timeline are the custom layer.

## What not to copy literally
Do not copy black/yellow. Do not copy sidebar/buttons.

## Transferable rule
Product mechanics should become custom visual language.

## RaiOS relevance
Audit/demo/outreach/follow-up can be visualized as a path if done elegantly.
